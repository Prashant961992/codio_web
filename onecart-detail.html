Case study detail

We build a very popular south african ecommerce platform that offers a convenient and seamless shopping experience to customers across the country. One of the key factors of this was the ability to offer customers a seamless shopping experience across multiple retailers. To achieve this, the platform uses an API-driven approach to integrate with various retailers' systems and pull their product catalogues into their database. Shop for groceries, pharmacy, baby, pet, wellness and liquor items from your favourite supermarkets online through the OneCart app and have your order delivered at your doorstep in as little as an hour! The website is built on a microservices architecture, which enables it to be highly scalable and flexible. The platform also uses machine learning algorithms to personalise the shopping experience for each customer.

Customers can also manage their orders, track their deliveries, and modify their account information through the website's user-friendly dashboard. Additionally, the site accepts a variety of payment options, including credit cards, debit cards, and electronic funds transfers (EFTs).

Overall, it is a well-designed and highly functional ecommerce platform that provides clients with a seamless buying experience. The platform's usage of modern technologies, such as microservices, machine learning, and API integration, has enabled it to provide its customers with a highly scalable and flexible solution.

Case study detail

A residential property management company that offers furnished apartments for short-term stays in a variety of places across the United States. There were other functionalities in this, such as managing building and residence access using virtual keys, chatting with the concierge for any customer needs, managing your reservation and exploring the local scene surrounding the property.

The fundamental and highly distinctive feature that we created on this platform was virtual keys. Instead of a standard physical key, guests are given a one-of-a-kind virtual key that is sent via the platform.

When the guest arrives at the property, they just open the app, choose their reservation, and unlock the door with their virtual key. The virtual key is sent to the smart lock on the door, which validates the key and then unlocks the door. The virtual key is set to only work during the guest's reservation term and is deactivated immediately when it expires. This guarantees that only authorised individuals have access to the room and that the item is kept safe. This increases security and convenience for visitors because they can reach their accommodation straight from the platform.

We developed a Virtual Key Management System for generating and storing unique virtual keys for each guest. It most likely use robust encryption techniques to safeguard sensitive data and ensure that keys cannot be readily intercepted or hacked. We created smart locks to work with the virtual key system. The locks interface with guests' mobile devices using Bluetooth and NFC technologies to validate virtual keys.To manage key generation, authentication, and other activities, the virtual key system is likely to rely on a powerful backend infrastructure. To ensure that guests can access their rooms simply and securely, the system depends on modern programming languages, safe encryption methods, and cloud-based infrastructure.

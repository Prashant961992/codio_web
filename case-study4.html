Case study detail

In the Arab World, we created an interactive school counselling platform that aims to promote behaviour by increasing the human values of the school through contact between the school and home. It enables the school's Guidance Department to create instructional policies, strategies, and behavioural evaluation methods. The program is an educational method for the conduct of school behaviour that is based on the awarding of behavioural points from instructors and parents of children who are then divided into groups based on their behaviour.
